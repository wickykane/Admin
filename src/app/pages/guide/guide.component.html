Demo Guideline

Scope of demo:
  1. Sel2B Backend:
    • Products Management.
    • Sales Management.
    • Customer Management
    • Financial Management.
  2. Sel2B Storefront:
    • Customer profile.
    • Customer buys/re-orders products online.
    • Customer participates in promotion event. System calculates the promotional products.
    • Customer checkout and creates the order from Sel2B Storefront to Sel2B Backend.
  3. Sel2B Supplier Portal:
    • A portal where suppliers can manage their purchase orders, quotes and products while in business with NABP
